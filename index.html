The Goal of the CCRA


The goal of the CCRA is to purchase, assemble and continuely operate a full size (63’ by 28’) playing field for area teams to practice for competition. The long term plan is for the field to be centrally located at Tunxis Community College in Farmington. Our vision is for the field to become a resource for local FIRST teams. In addition, our partnership with Tunxis would be a model for future affiliations between FIRST teams and community colleges.


What's it for? Is it Battle Bots?


(Stolen from FRC Site as placeholder) Under strict rules, limited time and resources, teams of students are challenged to raise funds, design a team "brand," hone teamwork skills, and build and program industrial-size robots to play a difficult field game against like-minded competitors. It’s as close to real-world engineering as a student can get. Volunteer professional mentors lend their time and talents to guide each team.
